How it works
Three steps.
Any plan.
01

Name your Scheme

Type it in plain language. Schemester extracts dates, destination, headcount, vibe — automatically.

02

Pick components

AI breaks your Scheme into venue, catering, outfits, travel — whatever fits. Swap, poll, or let AI decide.

03

Split & enjoy

Invite the group. Chat, vote, pay together or separately. Schemester tracks deadlines and price drops.

Features
Built for groups.

Live deal engine

Schemester searches the web in real-time for the best price within reach of your venue.

Group vote

Push any component to the group. Swipe to decide. Majority wins and auto-books.

Split Pay

Pay individually or as a group. Receipts auto-allocated, no more spreadsheets.

Scheme Rewind

Dashboard tracking every Scheme, pound saved, person planned-with. Share it yearly.
