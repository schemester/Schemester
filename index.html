Core Features

Everything you need to plan perfectly

Schemester combines AI intelligence, real-time availability search, and seamless group collaboration into one stunning platform.

✦
Schemy AI Assistant
Describe your event in plain language. Schemy creates a tailored planning checklist broken into every component — venues, catering, entertainment, attire, and more — in seconds.
🔍
Live Availability Search
Real-time web search surfaces the best vendors with live pricing, actual availability for your dates, and proximity to your venue. No stale listings, ever.
👥
Collaborative Planning
Invite co-planners, guests, and vendors into your Scheme. Vote on options, assign tasks, react to ideas — everything in a shared, real-time space.
💳
Group & Split Payments
Pay individually, split evenly, or collect contributions. Manage deposits, track balances, and send automatic reminders — no awkward money conversations needed.
📊
Smart Dashboard
A beautiful command centre tracking your planning progress, budget breakdown, vendor bookings, upcoming milestones, and everything your collaborators are up to.
🏆
Rewards & Scheme Points
Earn Scheme Points on every booking. Unlock badges, climb tiers from Novice Schemer to Legendary Schemer, and redeem points for discounts and exclusive vendor perks.

Ideas & Roadmap

What makes Schemester unstoppable

Beyond the core product — high-impact features that will differentiate Schemester from anything else on the market.

🗺️
Inspiration Feed
Browse trending public Schemes, save ideas to a personal Mood Board, and share your completed events to inspire others. Think Pinterest, but built for booking — with every item shoppable.
🎯
Price Alerts & Match
Set a target price for any vendor or booking. Get instant push notifications the moment availability opens up or the price drops. Smart waitlisting that auto-books when conditions are met.
📱
Guest Experience App
Share a Scheme link with guests — they get a beautiful personal event page with RSVP, dietary preferences, travel info, a digital invite, and a shared photo gallery post-event.
🏪
Vendor Marketplace Portal
A dedicated B2B portal for vendors to list services, manage live availability, receive instant booking requests, reply to enquiries, and grow their business via Schemester's network.
🌍
Destination Event Planner
Full destination support with local vendor discovery in any country, currency conversion, timezone-aware reminders, and integrated travel logistics for flights and accommodation.
🎮
Gamification & Tiers
Earn badges like "First Scheme", "Master Planner", and "Group Legend". Progress through tiers — Bronze → Gold → Legendary Schemer. Redeem points for vendor discounts and premium features.
🤖
AI Negotiation Agent
Schemy can reach out to vendors on your behalf to negotiate quotes, request custom packages, or ask about discounts — all with your approval before anything is agreed.
🌦️
Weather & Risk Intelligence
Live weather forecasts integrated into your Scheme timeline. Automated alerts for outdoor events with AI-recommended contingency vendors (marquees, indoor alternatives) pre-loaded.
🧾
Expense & Tax Reporting
Automated expense reports for corporate events and business occasions. Export a full spending breakdown, receipts, and invoices for accounting or reimbursement in one click.
Build Strategy

How to bring Schemester to life

A modern, scalable tech stack that powers AI search, real-time collaboration, and seamless payments from day one.

📱 Frontend & Apps
Next.js 15 (Web)
React-based with server rendering for fast load times and SEO. Powers the marketing site and web app.
React Native / Expo (Mobile)
Single codebase for iOS and Android. Share business logic with the web app for faster iteration.
Tailwind CSS
Rapid UI development with a consistent design system across all surfaces.
Framer Motion
Premium micro-animations and page transitions that give Schemester its premium feel.
⚙️ Backend & Infrastructure
Node.js + Fastify API
High-throughput REST + GraphQL API layer. Handles real-time events via WebSockets for live collaboration.
PostgreSQL + Supabase
Relational data model for schemes, users, vendors, bookings. Supabase adds real-time subscriptions and row-level security out of the box.
Redis
Cache layer for live search results, session management, and pub/sub messaging for real-time chat.
Vercel + Railway
Vercel for the Next.js frontend (edge functions, global CDN). Railway for the Node API and background workers.
✦ AI & Search
Claude API (Anthropic)
Powers Schemy — scheme creation, checklist generation, vendor recommendations, negotiation drafts, and conversational planning.
Perplexity / Tavily Search API
Live web search for real vendor prices, availability, and reviews — grounded in current data, not stale databases.
Google Maps Platform
Proximity search, venue maps, travel time estimates, and destination discovery for location-based filtering.
Pinecone (Vector DB)
Store embeddings of vendor profiles and past schemes to power semantic search and personalised AI recommendations.
💳 Payments & Comms
Stripe Connect
Handles split payments, group contributions, vendor payouts, and deposit management. The industry standard for marketplace payments.
Resend + React Email
Beautiful transactional emails for booking confirmations, payment reminders, and guest invitations.
Twilio / OneSignal
SMS reminders for time-sensitive bookings and push notifications for mobile — price alerts, payment reminders, and collaborator activity.
Clerk (Auth)
Social login (Google, Apple, Facebook), magic links, and multi-user invite flows — all production-ready from day one.
